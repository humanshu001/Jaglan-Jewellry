Exchange Policy



Our Exchange Policy


At Jaglan Jewellers, we are committed to providing you with a pleasant shopping experience. We understand that you might want to exchange your purchase for various reasons, and we're here to assist you.



Exchange Guidelines


  • Items eligible for exchange must be in their original condition and packaging.
  • Proof of purchase is required for all exchanges.
  • Exchanges must be initiated within 30 days of the purchase date.


How to Initiate an Exchange


To initiate an exchange, please contact our customer support team at customersupport@jaglanjewellers.com. Provide your order number and a brief description of the item you wish to exchange and the reason for the exchange.



Exchange Process


Once we receive the item you wish to exchange and inspect it, we will process your exchange. You may choose an alternative item from our collection of equal or higher value, and any price difference will be charged accordingly. Exchanged items will be shipped out after the inspection and payment, if applicable.



Contact Us


If you have any questions or concerns about our exchange policy, please don't hesitate to contact us.
