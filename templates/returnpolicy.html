Return Policy



Our Return Policy


At Jaglan Jewellers, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied for any reason, you may return the item within 30 days of the purchase date.



Return Guidelines


  • Items must be in their original condition and packaging.
  • Proof of purchase is required for all returns.
  • Returns must be initiated within 30 days of the purchase date.


How to Initiate a Return


To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team at customersupport@jaglanjewellers.com. Please provide your order number and a brief description of the reason for the return.



Refund Process


Once we receive your returned item and inspect it, we will process your refund. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 7 business days of receiving the returned item.



Contact Us


If you have any questions or concerns about our return policy, please don't hesitate to contact us.
