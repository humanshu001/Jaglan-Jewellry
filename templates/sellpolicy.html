Selling Jewelry Policy



Our Process for Selling Your Jewelry


At Jaglan Jewellers, we offer you the opportunity to sell your old jewelry and receive a fair value for it. Our process is designed to make selling your jewelry easy and hassle-free.



Assessment and Valuation


When you bring in your jewelry for selling, our experienced experts will assess its quality and value. We consider factors such as metal purity, gemstone quality, craftsmanship, and market trends.



Transparent Pricing


Our valuation process is transparent, and we'll provide you with a clear explanation of how we arrived at the offer. You can trust that you'll receive a competitive price based on the current market conditions.



Offer Acceptance


Once we present you with our offer, you have the choice to accept or decline it. If you choose to accept, we'll guide you through the necessary paperwork and documentation.



Payment Process


Upon completing the required paperwork, we'll process your payment using the agreed-upon method. You can expect prompt payment, ensuring a seamless selling experience.



Contact Us


If you're interested in selling your jewelry or have any questions about our selling process, please reach out to our dedicated customer support team at customersupport@jaglanjewellers.com.
